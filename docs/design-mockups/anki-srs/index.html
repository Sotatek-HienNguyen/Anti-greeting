Resilient

/rɪˈzɪl.jənt/ ADJECTIVE
Able to withstand or recover quickly from difficult conditions.

Common Collocations

  • Resilient economy
  • Remarkably resilient
  • Highly resilient

Word Family

  • Noun: Resilience
  • Adverb: Resiliently

Kiên cường, mau phục hồi

Khả năng nhanh chóng vượt qua những khó khăn hoặc nghịch cảnh; có tính bền bỉ trước áp lực.

Contextual Usage

  • "The community proved to be resilient in the face of the economic crisis."
  • "She has a resilient personality that allows her to bounce back from failure."
  • "This species of plant is remarkably resilient to changing weather patterns."

Synonyms

Buoyant Strong Irrepressible

Nuance Note

Unlike "strong," resilient emphasizes the ability to return to an original state after deformation or stress.
